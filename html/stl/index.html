Help

This map shows crime frequency in St. Louis City from January 1, 2013 through May 31, 2013.

Each square on the grid is colored according to how many crimes were reported to have occurred there. The legend in the bottom right of the map can be used to interpret these colors.

About

Data on crimes from the St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department, and retrieved using the CrimeDB API.

Please send questions or comments to info@crimedb.org.
