FAQ

What do un-colored squares on the map mean?

Squares on the map that have no color indicate that we don't have any data for that area. This is different than areas for which we know that no crimes have occurred -- these areas are rendered in blue.

What crimes are represented on the map?

All reported crimes from January 1 2014 - January 1 2015.

Why isn't there any data in my area?

This is most likely because CrimeDB doesn't know how to collect data from your local police department. Send mail to info@crimedb.org or tweet at @pgriess to tell me what's missing and I'll try to get it added.

Where does CrimeDB get its data?

Our data is automatically compiled from police jurisdictions from around the country. It is made available to anyone for use using the CrimeDB API.
