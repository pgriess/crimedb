CrimeDB

CrimeDB is an easy-to-use database of crime incidents from around the USA.

Citizens can use CrimeDB to view information about crime activity in their area, including which areas are more affected than others. Developers can use CrimeDB data in their applications with its easy-to-use API. See the developers page for more information.

For more information, contact info@crimedb.org or @pgriess.
